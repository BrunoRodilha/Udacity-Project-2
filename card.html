Blue Whale

The blue whale is the largest animal known to have ever lived!

  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus
  • Average Leght: 24 meters
  • Average lifespan: 80 - 110 years
  • Habitat: Open sea

The Blue Whale is the largest mammal on the planet, it is carnivorous and inhabits all the oceans of the Earth. At present he is in danger of instinct because of hunting with the purpose of obtaining his oil, for the industry of perumes, and his meat to be served as gastronomic delicacy
